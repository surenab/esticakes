Our Story

We are The EstiCake Bakery. We believe in whole foods and produce everything we sell from scratch here in Cork. We bake true artisan cakes without any unnecessary additives. We only use free range Irish eggs, real Irish butter and dairy products

Since our humble beginnings in 2018 we have grown to employ 4 people. Our wholefoods background and the fact we produce everything ourselves means we have an extensive range of products catering specifically to people with specialist dietary requirements. Nutrition and health are a big part of what we do and providing produce and advice to people on wheat-free, sugar-free, gluten-free, high-fibre, high-protein, vegetarian or vegan diets is our speciality.
